About Me

Hi, I'm Rowan! I'm a 16 year old amateur astrophotographer. I have always been interested in astronomy and am hoping for a career in it one day. For now, I can enjoy writing blogs, showing newcomers what it's like to first get into this fun hobby. As an amateur myself, I'm the perfect person to do this! I'm also working on an "astrophotographers best friend" for all skill levels, an ai assistant to help with anything you need, from planning your perfect stargazing night to helping you set up or troubleshoot your equipment. To host the site and help it grow, contributions of any size are welcome, all donations will go directly into the site and you will added the contributors list. Any feedback is gratefully appreciated.

Clear skies,

-Rowan
